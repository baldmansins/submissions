Waniga Feast 1

Location: Garden City Upper Food Court

Date: 19 June 2024

One for the ages. Waniga Feast 1 was the event that started it all — a legendary gathering that quickly became one of the greatest feasts of all time. Bringing together multiple friend groups from Mansfield State High, it created unforgettable memories, sparked new friendships, and set the standard for every feast that followed. It wasn’t just a celebration; it was the foundation of a community united by good food, laughter, and shared experiences.

Over $200 was spent on this feast solely on Friend Chicken from KFC and the legendary late O'Chicken. Additionally, multiple bowls of chips were purchased from Mr Curry aswell a HSP from Origin Kebabs.

Waniga Feast 2

Location: Garden City Upper Food Court

Date: 11 September 2024

Waniga Feast 2 was a pay-to-enter event that, while not quite living up to the legendary status of Waniga Feast 1, still carved out its place in history. Bringing friends together once again, it offered great food, laughs, and memories, proving that the spirit of the Waniga Feasts was very much alive — even if the first one set an almost impossible standard.

Despite the ticketed entry, the event maintained a lively and welcoming atmosphere. People mingled across groups, tried new foods, and celebrated in a way that reinforced the Waniga Feast tradition. Though it may not have reached the peak of the first, Feast 2 proved that the legacy was continuing, building anticipation for the feasts yet to come.

Pereira Feast

Location: The Pereira's

Date: 21 March 2026

Waniga didn’t just eat amongst us. He feasted, and in doing so, carved his name into something no distance, no departure, no passage of time could ever erase. Men Move. Legends don’t. Table changes. Faces rotate. But the spirit of the Feast? Never. Waniga Feast is not merely a memory. It’s an institution. A spoken name at every table, honoured at every plate, felt in every moment where life is pushed beyond bounds. Waniga showed us what’s capable when you exert energy to that magnitude. It ceases to belong to a moment, yet begins to cement in time. Forever. So let us rise up in honour. As somewhere out there, Wanigas plate is full….and so is his legacy.
